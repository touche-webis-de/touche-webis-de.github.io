Synopsis/Call

TODO Making informed decisions and forming opinions on a matter often involves not only weighing pro and con arguments towards different options, but also considering cause-effect relationships for one's actions. Our goal is to foster and support the development of technologies for argument retrieval and analysis. We invite you to participate in the 5th Touché lab on argument and causal retrieval at CLEF 2024 featuring two tasks.

Shared Tasks

  • Image Retrieval for Arguments
  • Human Value Detection

Important Dates

  • September, 2023: Initial data release.
  • November, 2023: Registration open.

All deadlines are 23:59 CEST.
